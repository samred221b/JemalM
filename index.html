About Our Company

Your Trusted Partner in Overseas Employment

Jemal Mohammed Foreign Employment Agency is a government-approved recruitment agency based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We are dedicated to connecting skilled Ethiopian workers with legitimate overseas employment opportunities in the Gulf region.

With over a decade of experience in international recruitment, we have successfully placed thousands of workers in rewarding positions across Kuwait and Saudi Arabia. Our commitment to ethical recruitment practices and worker welfare has made us one of the most trusted agencies in Ethiopia.

We understand that seeking employment abroad is a significant life decision. That's why we provide comprehensive support throughout your entire journey - from initial application and training to deployment and ongoing support in your destination country. Our team of experienced professionals ensures that every worker is well-prepared, properly documented, and matched with reputable employers who respect worker rights.

As a fully licensed agency by the Ethiopian Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs, we operate in strict compliance with both Ethiopian and international labor laws. We believe in transparency, fair treatment, and creating opportunities that truly change lives.

Our Recruitment Process

We guide you through every step of your journey to overseas employment

1

Application & Registration

Submit your application online or visit our office. Provide required documents and complete registration forms.

Duration: 1-2 days
2

Screening & Interview

Initial assessment of qualifications, skills verification, and personal interview to understand your preferences.

Duration: 3-5 days
3

Job Matching

We connect you with verified employers that match your skills, experience, and career goals.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
4

Documentation & Visa

Complete document legalization, medical tests, and visa application processing with full support.

Duration: 4-6 weeks

Pre-Departure Training
