About Our Company

Your Trusted Partner in Overseas Employment

Jemal Mohammed Ahmed Foreign Employment Agency is a government-approved recruitment agency based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We are dedicated to connecting skilled Ethiopian workers with legitimate overseas employment opportunities in the Gulf region.

With over a decade of experience in international recruitment, we have successfully placed thousands of workers in rewarding positions across Kuwait and Saudi Arabia. Our commitment to ethical recruitment practices and worker welfare has made us one of the most trusted agencies in Ethiopia.

As a fully licensed agency by the Ethiopian Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs, we operate in strict compliance with both Ethiopian and international labor laws. We believe in transparency, fair treatment, and creating opportunities that truly change lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recruitment services

Why Choose Us?

  • Over 15 years of experience
  • 5000+ successful placements
  • Licensed & verified agency
  • 24/7 support throughout your journey
  • Transparent pricing, no hidden fees
  • Partnerships with top Middle East employers

Which countries do you recruit for?

We specialize in recruitment for the Middle East, including Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain. We also have connections in other countries and can assist with international placements based on your preferences.

Do you provide training and orientation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive pre-departure training including cultural orientation, language basics, workplace etiquette, and job-specific skills training. We also offer ongoing support throughout your employment contract.

What if I'm not satisfied with the job?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your placement, we work with you to find a more suitable position. We also provide contract reviews and mediation services to ensure fair treatment from employers.

How do you ensure worker safety and rights?

We partner only with verified, reputable employers who comply with labor laws. We conduct thorough background checks and provide 24/7 support. Our team monitors working conditions and helps resolve any issues that arise during employment.
